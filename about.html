The JuneYao Story

FROM THE SKY TO THE ROAD

JuneYao Group began as an airline. Today, we bring that same engineering precision and passenger-first philosophy to electric mobility.

The Group

JUNEYAO GROUP

Founded in 2005, Juneyao Airlines rapidly grew into one of China's leading commercial carriers, renowned for safety, technology, and passenger experience. The JuneYao Group's portfolio spans aviation, finance, consumer goods, and education.

In 2023, JuneYao Group entered the automotive industry with a bold ambition: to bring the comfort, safety, and technology of first-class air travel to everyday driving. JuneYao Auto was born.

Our Timeline

THE JOURNEY

2005

Juneyao Airlines Founded

Shanghai-based Juneyao Airlines launches as a private commercial carrier, growing to one of China's top domestic and international airlines.

2023

JuneYao Auto Established

JuneYao Group acquires Yudo Auto and establishes JuneYao Automobile, bringing aviation engineering DNA to the electric vehicle industry.

2024

JY AIR Launches in Thailand

The JY AIR makes its global debut at Thailand Motor Expo 2024 — the first JuneYao-branded electric vehicle, earning 5-star NCAP & E-NCAP safety ratings.

2025

Arriving in Malaysia

JuneYao Auto expands to Malaysia, bringing the JY AIR to Kuala Lumpur with local pricing, full after-sales support, and an exclusive launch offer.

Our Philosophy

THREE PILLARS

Everything JuneYao Auto builds is guided by three core engineering and design principles inherited from our aviation heritage.

SAFETY FIRST

Engineered on the SKY platform with aerospace-grade structural integrity. 5-star NCAP and E-NCAP certified. Because nothing matters more than the people inside.

INTELLIGENT DESIGN

Crystal OS, Level 2+ autonomous driving, Snapdragon 8155 — the JY AIR is as smart as the devices in your pocket, and smarter on the road.

FIRST CLASS COMFORT

Inspired by business class cabins. A 2m² panoramic roof, 75% soft-touch interior, reclining seats, ambient lighting. Your commute, elevated.
